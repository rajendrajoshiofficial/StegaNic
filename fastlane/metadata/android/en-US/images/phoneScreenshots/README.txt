Place your app screenshots here for F-Droid listing.

FILE NAMING (must be sequential):
  1.png  - Encode Screen (main screen with file picker)
  2.png  - Decoding Screen
  3.png  - Settings Screen
  4.png  - Loading/Cyberpunk animation (optional)

REQUIREMENTS:
  - Format: PNG or JPG
  - Resolution: minimum 320px wide, max 3840px
  - Aspect ratio: Portrait (9:16 recommended)
  - Count: At least 1, max 8 screenshots

HOW TO TAKE SCREENSHOTS:
  1. Connect your phone (USB debugging ON)
  2. Open Android Studio -> "Logcat" dropdown -> your device
  3. Or: Android Studio -> "Device Manager" -> Screenshot button
  Note: App has FLAG_SECURE set. Use Android Studio's built-in
        screenshot tool instead of the phone's power+volume button.
